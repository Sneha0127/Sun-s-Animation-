What is decryption?

  • Decryption is the process of transforming data that has been rendered unreadable through encryption back to its unencrypted form.
  • In decryption, the system extracts and converts the garbled data and transforms it to texts and images that are easily understandable not only by the reader but also by the system.
  • Decryption may be accomplished manually or automatically.
  • One of the foremost reasons for implementing an encryption-decryption system is privacy.
  • The person in charge of decryption receives a prompt or window in which a key may be entered to access encrypted information.
